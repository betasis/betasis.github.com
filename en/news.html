Olympus Automation Meeting

27-February-2014



We contacted to Olympus Automation company , when we were searching new technologies for our market. They came here to grow their market to Turkey.


Olympus Automation is an inventor company about food processing technologies. You can found information for them in their web site. They are producing turnkey solutions for liquid food and powder food processing plants. This internnational company makes lots of projects in countries around the world. We wonder their steaminfusion cooking system , and Factory Performance teknolojileri ile ilgilendik. Bu teknolojilere müşterilerimizin ihtiyacı olduğunu düşünüyoruz.


Jake Norman and Mark Beaver from Olympus Automation company came to here for a meeting. We told them Betasis company and capabilities. We visited some customers together. We showed our realized projects them and they presented their products to our customers.



We have done Anderson-Negele training

10-April-2014



Our works about Anderson-Negele official Turkey distributorship are continuing. Anderson-Negele produces high quality hygienic sensors for food and pharma industries.


Nikolas Städele is Market Development Manager in Anderson-Negele company. We are always meeting him from our first contact. This week Mr. Nikolas Städele visited our company to clear our cooperation in Turkey. Also he came here to give training to us about products. In this 2 days training we learned tecnical details about all Anderson-Negele products. We also learned strategies for customer relationships and some products which has no competitors.


After our first hard workings , of course we have plenty of fun. We showed nice places of Bursa city to Mr. Städele , and ate nice foods together. We hope our first sales as an official distributor will be soon.
